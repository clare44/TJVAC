Donations

On this page, you will find organizations to donate too who are either shelters or transitional hosuing for LGBTQ+ homless youth.

The 519

The 519 is a Toronto based organization that supports LGBTQ+ communities and youth with many things including helping them find shelter.

  • To donate to the 519 follow the link: https://www.the519.org/support-the-519/donate

    • Eva's House

    Eva's house has three locations across Toronto where they provide safe shelter and transitional housing for approximatley 123 youth every night. They support LGBTQ+ youth, anong many others, have a safe place to spend the night and helps the get back on their feet.

  • To donate to Eva's House, follow the link: https://www.evas.ca/get-involved/ways-to-donate

    • Friends of Ruby

    Friends of Ruby is an organization based in Toronto that provides transitiona; housing for LGBTQ+ youth. Their goal is to provide a safe space for LGBTQ+ youth and to get them on a path to independant, stable, and impowered living.

  • To donate to Friends of Ruby, follow the link: https://donate.micharity.com/friends-of-ruby/3556835139/donate?campaign=1

    • YMCA Sprott House

    The YMCA Sprott House opened in 2007 and was one of the first transitional housing programs in Canada for LGBTQ+ youth. They are based in Toronto and provide up to a year of housing for youth in need of somewhere to stay.

  • To donate to the YMCA Sprott House, follow this link: https://ymcagta.org/support-the-y/donate-now
